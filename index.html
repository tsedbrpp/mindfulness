Top 10 Mindfulness Apps

1. Headspace

Headspace offers hundreds of guided meditations on everything from stress management to sleep enhancement. The app features mindfulness exercises suitable for both beginners and experienced practitioners.

Visit Headspace

2. Calm

Calm provides a variety of guided meditations, sleep stories, and relaxing music tracks. It aims to help users manage stress, anxiety, and improve their sleep quality.

Visit Calm

3. Insight Timer

Insight Timer boasts a large library of free meditations led by renowned mindfulness experts. It also features a customizable meditation timer and community features.

Visit Insight Timer

4. Ten Percent Happier

Designed for skeptics, Ten Percent Happier offers straightforward meditations and talks to help users reduce stress and increase focus.

Visit Ten Percent Happier

5. Buddhify

Buddhify offers mindfulness exercises that you can do anywhere, anytime. The app categorizes meditations based on what you're doing or how you're feeling.

Visit Buddhify

6. Simple Habit

Simple Habit provides 5-minute meditations for busy people. It covers various topics like stress, relationships, and even specific situations like commuting.

Visit Simple Habit

7. Unwinding Anxiety

Unwinding Anxiety® is an app-based program clinically proven to reduce anxiety through guided lessons, mindfulness exercises, and community support.

Visit Unwinding Anxiety

8. Waking Up with Sam Harris

Waking Up combines the wisdom of neuroscience and philosophy to help users build a meditation practice. The app offers guided meditations and lessons.

Visit Waking Up

9. Aura

Aura offers personalized meditation tracks based on your mood and preferences. It also includes life coaching sessions and nature sounds.

Visit Aura

10. Smiling Mind

Developed by psychologists, Smiling Mind offers mindfulness programs for people of all ages, including dedicated programs for children and teenagers.

Visit Smiling Mind
